Super Raitha

About the Dish

Raita is a side dish and condiment in Indian cuisine made of dahi (yogurt, often referred to as curd) together with raw or cooked vegetables, fruit, or, in the case of boondi raita, with fried droplets of batter made from besan (chickpea flour, generally labeled as gram flour).

List of Ingredients

  • Yogurt(Curd)
  • Cumin Powder
  • Green Chillies
  • Coriander Leaves
  • Onion

Procedure

  1. Take the Curd into a bowl and whisk it gently till it gains some texture
  2. Add in the Cumin powder, Chopped Green Chillies, Coriander leaves and Onion
  3. Whisk again gently
  4. Add in 100 ml chilled water and mix
  5. Serve chilled as a side dish
